## Transitions

### Propositional Logic

A' <=> (B & C & D) | (A & ~B & ~C) | (A & ~B & D)
B’ <=> (~A & B) | (~A & C & ~D)
C’ <=> (~B & C & ~D) | ( ~A & B & D) | (B & C & D) | (A & ~B & ~D)
D’ <=> (~A & B & ~C) | ( ~A & B & D) | (A & C & D)
